Preston Idaho

The Preston location has top-notch schools, low unemployment, first-rate medical personnel, an extraordinary fitness care facility, town and county governments, unsurpassed scenic beauty, outside recreation, and loads of cultural sports for the complete family.

Napoleon Dynamite

Preston is well-known for the hit movie “Napoleon Dynamite“. Preston, Idaho has become a traveler enchantment for fans. People come from everywhere in the globe to take an excursion. You can visit the locations featured in the Napoleon Dynamite movie.
